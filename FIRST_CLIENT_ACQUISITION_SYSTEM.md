## 📞 3. CLOSING CALL SCRIPT

### CALL STRUCTURE (15-30 minutes)

#### OPENING: Build Rapport (2 minutes)
```
"Hi [Name], thanks for taking the time to speak with me today.

Quick question before we dive in: What's one thing that's working really well for [Company] right now, and one thing that's challenging?"

[Listen actively, take notes]
```

#### DISCOVERY: Understand Their Situation (5-7 minutes)
```
"I work with companies who are looking to reduce customer service costs while maintaining quality.

Can you tell me about your current customer service setup?
- How many agents do you have?
- What's your current cost per hour?
- What are your biggest pain points?
- What's your monthly customer service budget?"

[Listen for budget, timeline, pain points]
```

#### VALUE PROPOSITION: Present Solution (3-5 minutes)
```
"Based on what you've shared, here's how we typically help companies like [Company]:

CURRENT SITUATION:
- [X] agents at $[Y]/hour = $[Z] monthly
- Pain points: [repeat their challenges]

OUR SOLUTION:
- Same quality service at $[A]/hour (60% savings)
- Tunisia-based team with [relevant language] fluency
- European timezone alignment
- 30-day money-back guarantee

This typically saves companies $[amount] monthly."
```

#### SOCIAL PROOF: Build Credibility (2 minutes)
```
"We've helped [similar company] in [their industry] save $50K monthly.

They were facing [similar challenge] and now have [benefit].

Would you like me to share their contact info as a reference?"
```

#### QUALIFICATION: Budget & Timeline (2 minutes)
```
"To make sure we're a good fit, can I ask:

What's your monthly budget range for this?
What's your timeline for implementation?
Who else needs to be involved in the decision?"

[If budget too low or timeline too long, politely end]
```

#### CLOSE: Get Commitment (3 minutes)
```
"Based on our conversation, it sounds like we could save [Company] $[amount] monthly.

Would you like to move forward with a detailed proposal?

We can start with a small pilot project to test the waters, or go full implementation.

Which approach interests you more?"

[Handle objections, get specific next steps]
```

#### NEXT STEPS: Clear Action Items
```
"Great! Here's what happens next:

1. I'll send you a detailed proposal within 24 hours
2. We'll schedule a call with your team to discuss implementation
3. Pilot starts within 2 weeks if you approve

Does that timeline work for you?"
```

### COMMON OBJECTIONS & RESPONSES

**"We're happy with our current provider"**
→ "That's great to hear. What do you like most about them? Many clients tell me they stay because they haven't explored better alternatives."

**"Cost is our main concern"**
→ "I completely understand. Let me show you our pricing vs. European providers. We save 60% while maintaining quality."

**"We need to think about it"**
→ "Of course. What specific concerns do you have? I'd love to address them now so you can make an informed decision."

**"Send me information"**
→ "Absolutely. I'll send a detailed proposal with case studies and pricing. When would be a good time for a follow-up call?"

## 🎯 4. LEAD QUALIFICATION FRAMEWORK

### QUALIFICATION CRITERIA

#### MUST-HAVES (No-Go if missing)
- **Budget:** $5,000+/month outsourcing spend
- **Timeline:** Decision within 3 months
- **Authority:** Decision-maker or key influencer
- **Need:** Current pain with customer service costs

#### NICE-TO-HAVES (Bonus points)
- **Industry Fit:** E-commerce, SaaS, healthcare, finance
- **Company Size:** 50-500 employees
- **Location:** Europe, UAE (cultural fit)
- **Urgency:** Current provider issues or growth plans

### QUALIFICATION SCORE (1-10)

**Score 8-10: PRIORITY LEAD**
- Budget: $10K+/month
- Timeline: Within 1 month
- Pain: Severe (high costs, quality issues)
- Authority: CEO/COO level

**Score 5-7: GOOD LEAD**
- Budget: $5K-10K/month
- Timeline: 1-3 months
- Pain: Moderate
- Authority: Operations/Manager level

**Score 1-4: LOW PRIORITY**
- Budget: <$5K/month
- Timeline: 3+ months
- Pain: Mild
- Authority: Low-level

### QUALIFICATION QUESTIONS

#### Budget Qualification
```
"What's your current monthly spend on customer service/admin tasks?"
"What's your budget range for outsourcing solutions?"
"Are you looking to maintain, reduce, or increase your current spend?"
```

#### Timeline Qualification
```
"What's your timeline for implementing changes?"
"Who needs to be involved in the decision process?"
"When would you like to have this operational?"
```

#### Authority Qualification
```
"Are you the final decision-maker for this?"
"Who else needs to approve this type of spending?"
"What's your role in the decision process?"
```

#### Need Qualification
```
"What's your biggest challenge with current customer service?"
"What results are you hoping to achieve?"
"Why are you considering outsourcing now?"
```

### LEAD STATUS SYSTEM

#### Status Levels
1. **Raw Lead:** Initial contact, no qualification
2. **Qualified:** Meets basic criteria, worth pursuing
3. **Hot:** High score, active interest, timeline clear
4. **Proposal Sent:** Customized proposal delivered
5. **Negotiation:** Terms being discussed
6. **Closed Won:** Contract signed
7. **Closed Lost:** Decided against us

#### Follow-up Cadence
- **Raw → Qualified:** Within 24 hours
- **Qualified → Hot:** Within 3 days
- **Hot → Proposal:** Within 1 week
- **Proposal → Negotiation:** Daily follow-up
- **Negotiation → Closed:** As needed
